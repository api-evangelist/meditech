<p><span>Our venture into wellness began with a prescription. Five years ago, one of our cardiologists wrote his patient a “prescription” to use our fitness complex to improve their cardiovascular health. Patients often expect their providers to prescribe a medication, which helps manage symptoms, whereas this direction got to the root. Our CEO recognized the potential of that concept, and the Prescription for Your Health (P4YH) program emerged from that idea.</span><br /></p>
