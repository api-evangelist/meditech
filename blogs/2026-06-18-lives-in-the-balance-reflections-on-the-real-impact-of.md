A MEDITECH product manager reflects on how the Traverse Exchange interoperability solution and the Consolidated Patient Summary tool are positively impacting clinicians and patients. Real-world examples from healthcare providers show how the technology streamlines access to external patient data, improves clinical decision-making, and helps save lives by breaking down data silos across healthcare systems.
