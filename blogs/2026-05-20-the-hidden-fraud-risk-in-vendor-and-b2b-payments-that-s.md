The most dangerous moment in hospital finance operations isn’t when the payment is released — it’s everything that comes before it. Every vendor and business-to-business (B2B) payment, patient refund, and accounts payable transaction represents a potential entry point for fraud. One compromised email, altered bank detail, or missed red flag can result in significant financial loss, operational disruption, and erosion of hard-earned trust.
