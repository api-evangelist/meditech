Howard Messing reflects on his 50-year journey at MEDITECH, from joining as a programmer in 1974 to being appointed Chair of the Board of Directors. He describes the remarkable evolution of computing technology during his career, from computers being science fiction concepts to becoming integral parts of everyday healthcare. The post offers a personal perspective on how MEDITECH grew alongside the transformation of health information technology over five decades.
